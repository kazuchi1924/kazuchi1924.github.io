GeoMemo Privacy Policy

1. Overview 2. Information We Collect 3. Purpose of Use 4. Sharing with Third Parties 5. Use of External Services 6. User Choices 7. Contact

1. Overview

GeoMemo (hereinafter referred to as “the App”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how the App handles information.

2. Information We Collect

The App stores the following information on your device based on your actions:

  • Location information (only when explicitly permitted by the user)
  • Photos
  • Voice memos (audio recordings)
  • Text memos
  • App settings (theme, font, etc.)

3. Purpose of Use

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Recording, viewing, and managing user-created memos
  • App customization (theme, font, etc.)
  • Backup functionality (only when explicitly initiated by the user)

4. Sharing with Third Parties

The App does not provide or share user information with any third parties.

5. Use of External Services

The App uses the following external services:

  • OpenStreetMap (map display and address information)
  • Google Maps (map display)
  • GSI Maps (Geospatial Information Authority of Japan) (map display)
Please refer to the respective official websites for the privacy policies of these services.

6. User Choices

  • Recording and location access occur only when explicitly initiated by the user.
  • You can modify the App’s permissions (microphone, location, etc.) from your device settings.
